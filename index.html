Welcome to the Brim Brothers & Sisters

The Server Rules

  • 1. Be Respectful: Treat everyone with respect. No bullying, harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • 2. Keep It Safe for Work (SFW): No inappropriate or NSFW content. Keep the server family-friendly.
  • 3. No Spamming: Avoid spamming messages or content. Keep chats clean.
  • 4. Do Not Invite Without Permission: Only invite with explicit permission from the Brim Leader.
  • 5. Follow the Brim Leader's Instructions: Listen and follow guidance carefully.
  • 6. Follow Discord’s Terms of Service: Comply with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  • 7. Use Channels Appropriately: Stick to the topic of each channel.
  • 8. No Self-Promotion or Advertising: Avoid promoting content without permission.
  • 9. Respect Privacy: Don’t share personal information without consent.
  • 10. Listen to Staff: Follow instructions of moderators and admins.
  • 11. Report Issues: Report rule violations to moderators privately.
  • 12. Have Fun and Stay Positive: Engage in a friendly and welcoming manner.
  • 13. Always Wear a Top Hat: Mandatory and a sign of respect to the community.
